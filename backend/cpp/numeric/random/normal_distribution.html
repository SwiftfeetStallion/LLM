according to the Normal (or Gaussian) random number distribution. It is defined as: \(\small{f(x;\mu,\sigma)}=\frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi} }\exp{(-\frac{1}{2}{(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma})}^2)}\)f(x; μ,σ) = 1σ√2π exp⎛⎜⎝-12 ⎛⎜⎝x-μσ⎞⎟⎠2⎞⎟⎠ Here \(\small\mu\)μ is the Mean and \(\small\sigma\)σ is the Standard deviation (stddev). std::normal_distribution satisfies all requirements of RandomNumberDistribution.

Does not invoke any move, copy, or swap operations on individual stacktrace_entry objects. All iterators and references remain valid. The end() iterator is invalidated. If std::allocator_traits::propagate_on_container_swap::value is true, then the allocators are exchanged using an unqualified call to non-member swap. Otherwise, they are not swapped (and if get_allocator() != other.get_allocator(), the behavior is undefined).

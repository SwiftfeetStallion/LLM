Types > struct hash>; (since C++17) The template specialization of std::hash for the std::variant template allows users to obtain hashes of variant objects. The specialization std::hash> is enabled (see std::hash) if every specialization in std::hash>... is enabled, and is disabled otherwise. The member functions of this specialization are not guaranteed to be noexcept.

The value of the counter is initialized on creation. Threads may block on the latch until the counter is decremented to zero. There is no possibility to increase or reset the counter, which makes the latch a single-use barrier. Unlike std::experimental::barrier, std::experimental::latch can be decremented by a participating thread more than once.

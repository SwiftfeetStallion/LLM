Args > iterator emplace_hint( const_iterator hint, Args&&... args ); (since C++23) (constexpr since C++26) Inserts a new element into the container as close as possible to the position just before hint. The constructor of value_type (i.e., std::pair) is called with exactly the same arguments as supplied to the function, forwarded with std::forward(args)....

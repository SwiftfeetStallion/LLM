Swaps the contents of lhs and rhs. Calls lhs.swap(rhs). This overload participates in overload resolution only if std::is_swappable_v is true. Exceptions noexcept specification:   noexcept(noexcept(lhs.swap(rhs))) (since C++17) Notes Although the overloads of std::swap for container adaptors are introduced in C++11, container adaptors can already be swapped by std::swap in C++98. Such calls to std::swap usually have linear time complexity, but better complexity may be provided.

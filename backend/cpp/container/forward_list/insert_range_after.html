Each iterator in the range rg is dereferenced exactly once. If any of the following conditions is satisfied, the behavior is undefined: T is not EmplaceConstructible into forward_list from *ranges::begin(rg). pos is not in the range [before_begin(), end()). rg and *this overlap. No iterators or references become invalidated.

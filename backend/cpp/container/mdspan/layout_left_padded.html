Every specialization of layout_left_padded is TriviallyCopyable, and std::is_trivially_default_constructible_v is true for any such specialization T. Let m be the instance of the mapping type layout_left_padded::mapping. The padding stride of the mapping is the second stride m.stride(1) which is guaranteed to be at least the first extent value m.extent(0), called extent to pad.

Merges the resulting sorted range and the sorted range of pre-existing elements into a single sorted range. Each iterator in the range rg is dereferenced exactly once. If any of the following conditions is satisfied, the behavior is undefined: value_type is not EmplaceConstructible into flat_multiset from *ranges::begin(rg) rg and *this overlap.

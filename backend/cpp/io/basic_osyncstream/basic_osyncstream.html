Move-constructs the std::basic_ostream base and the std::basic_syncbuf member from the corresponding subobjects of other, then calls set_rdbuf with the pointer to the newly-constructed underlying std::basic_syncbuf to complete the initialization of the base. After this move constructor, other.get_wrapped() returns nullptr and destruction of other produces no output.

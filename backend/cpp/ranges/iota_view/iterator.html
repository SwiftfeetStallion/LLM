Type I models advanceable only if I satisfies advanceable and all concepts it subsumes are modeled, and given objects a and b of type I and value n of type D, such that b is reachable from a after n applications of ++a, all following conditions are satisfied: (a += n) is equal to b. std::addressof(a += n) is equal to std::addressof(a).

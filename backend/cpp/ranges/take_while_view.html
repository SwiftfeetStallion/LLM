which the predicate returns false. 2) RangeAdaptorObject. The expression views::take_while(e, f) is expression-equivalent to take_while_view(e, f) for any suitable subexpressions e and f. take_while_view models the concepts contiguous_range, random_access_range, bidirectional_range, forward_range, and input_range when the underlying view V models respective concepts.

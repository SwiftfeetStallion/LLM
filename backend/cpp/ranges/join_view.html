1) A range adaptor that represents view consisting of the sequence obtained from flattening a view of ranges. 2) RangeAdaptorObject (and also RangeAdaptorClosureObject). The expression views::join(e) is expression-equivalent to join_view>{e} for any suitable subexpressions e. join_view models input_range. join_view models forward_range when: ranges::range_reference_t is a reference type, and V and ranges::range_reference_t each model forward_range. join_view models bidirectional_range when: ranges::range_reference_t is a reference type, V models bidirectional_range, and ranges::range_reference_t models both bidirectional_range and common_range. join_view models common_range when: ranges::range_reference_t is a reference type, and V and ranges::range_reference_t each model forward_range and common_range.

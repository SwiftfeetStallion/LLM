value. Can be either bounded or unbounded (infinite). 2) views::repeat(e) and views::repeat(e, f) are expression-equivalent to repeat_view>(e) and repeat_view(e, f) respectively for any suitable subexpressions e and f. 3) Determines whether a type is integer-like and has a usable difference type. repeat_view models random_access_range. If Bound is not std::unreachable_sentinel_t, repeat_view also models sized_range and common_range.
